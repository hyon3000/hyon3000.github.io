I. Polycube

3D Polycube Tetris. Stack blocks on a 7×7 floor and clear lines to score.

√(lines)x bonus. Lv ↑ every 800 pts

Game over if height exceeded.

II. Why Play

  • A way to pass time
  • May improve spatial awareness
  • Suggestions welcome
